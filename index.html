WHO WE ARE?


At the VR/AR/MR club, we are dedicated to pushing the envelope when it comes to bleeding edge technology. At our club we believe in creating a conducive environment for our members to let their creativity take the centre stage helping bridge the gap between industry and academia. The combination of teamwork, tenacity, passion and ability to think outside of the box is what seperates our club from others.

VR

Virtual World experience via VR Headsets. Nothing is Real.

AR

Virtual Models are augmented over the real world using camera and a display to mimic the fact that it is actually present there.

MR

The concept of AR, Just more advanced and interactable.

DOMAINS WE WORK ON

Development

Under development, our club offers web development, game development and app development using VR/AR and Web Technologies. Our team of developers is dedicated to creating meticulously designed websites, games and apps.

3D Modeling

3D Modeling is an ever-evolving field and we pride ourselves in creating realistic three dimensional models and bringing our imagination into reality.

Research & Innovation

At VR AR MR, we have always believed in research and development in any endeavor we have undertaken. We are constantly pushing the envelope of contributing to existing technologies.

UI/UX

UI/UX refers to user experience and user interface. Our UI/UX team is skilled in creating web designs, app designs, animations and creating the ideal user experience for ease of use.

Management

Our team of managers work round the clock to ensure the smooth operations of the club and the coordination and communication between the different teams.

Events' Gallery

Our club has hosted various events to make students aware of our mission and foster interest in Virtual reality and Augmented reality.

Most Recent Events

Tachyon 2023

At Tachyon, our club organised various games based on the VR/AR technology and conducted workshops and hackathons, helping students create their own filters emulating the likes of Snapchat and Facebook.

More Events from VR AR MR

METACON

First year students were given an hands on experience of the OCULUS Quest 2 and a treasure hunt was conducted to help them realise the wide applications of VR/AR technology.

ORIENTATION

First year students were introduced to our club and our vision and given a brief introduction to the relevant technologies. An interactive Q&A was held to answer all of their questions pertaining to the future of extended reality

SPARK AR

A workshop was held for the first year students where they were introduced to SPARK AR and the tools provided by Meta to create and share augmented reality experiences .
